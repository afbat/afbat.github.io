#### Zines & Community Resources

+ **[Local Online Community Moderation: A Research-Based Guide for Digital Community Leaders](/zine.pdf)** - This zine translates findings from NSF-funded research on volunteer moderators in local online communities. Created for community organizers, group administrators, and digital community leaders, the guide covers practical strategies for moderating place-based online groups, managing common challenges like scams and conflicts, and building thriving local information ecosystems. The zine synthesizes insights from interviews with moderators across the United States and offers actionable advice for those working to keep their communities connected and informed. Artwork by [Margalo Guo](https://margaloguo.com/); layout and design by Ava Francesca Battocchio. Licensed under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International.

      <h2>Local Online Community Moderation: A Research-Based Guide for Digital Community Leaders</h2>
    </header>
    <section class="entry-content">
      <p>This zine translates findings from NSF-funded research on volunteer moderators in local online communities. Created for community organizers, group administrators, and digital community leaders, the guide covers practical strategies for moderating place-based online groups, managing common challenges like scams and conflicts, and building thriving local information ecosystems. Artwork by <a href="https://margaloguo.com/">Margalo Guo</a>; layout and design by Ava Francesca Battocchio.</p>
      
      <h3>How to Print Your Zine</h3>
      <p>Open the <a href="/zine_selfprint.pdf">self-print PDF</a> in Preview and use these settings:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Paper size:</strong> 8.5 x 11 inches</li>
        <li><strong>Copies:</strong> Double-sided on</li>
        <li><strong>Layout:</strong> 2 pages per sheet</li>
        <li><strong>Paper orientation:</strong> Portrait</li>
        <li><strong>Layout direction:</strong> First option (left to right, top to bottom)</li>
      </ul>
      <p><em>Note: Some pages may appear upside down in the preview—this is intentional to ensure everything prints correctly when folded.</em></p>
